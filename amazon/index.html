Profit Trends

+

The Total Earned Profit fluctuates over time, with specific periods of high profitability. Identifying these growth peaks can help recognize factors contributing to success, such as promotions or product launches, while low periods indicate where improvements could be made.

Discount Impact

+

The Discount Issued shows a direct correlation with sales trends. Analyzing the relationship between discounts and the total quantity sold can reveal whether discount strategies are effectively boosting sales or eroding profit margins.

City Performance

+

    The Top Performing Cities chart indicates significant differences in sales growth between regions. Identifying the high-performing cities can guide targeted marketing efforts, while low-performing cities may highlight areas needing promotional support or product adjustments.

Shopping Mode

+

The Shopping Mode Donut Chart shows a breakdown of consumer preferences, with categories such as Economy, Economy Plus, Priority, and Intermediate. Understanding this data can help optimize product offerings and promotional strategies tailored to the most popular shopping modes.

Products & Customers

+

The Top Product and Top Customer bar charts provide insights into which products are driving the most sales and which customers are making the largest purchases. This can guide inventory management and personalized marketing for high-value customers.
