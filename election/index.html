Election Trends

+

This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of election details from 1977 to 2014, covering 11 election cycles. It tracks key trends such as voter turnout, party performance, and electoral shifts over the decades, offering historical insights into the changing political landscape.

Geographical Insights

+

A shape map visual displays district-level results, enabling users to explore regional differences and identify areas with significant electoral activity. This spatial representation highlights district performance across diverse regions.

Party Performance

+

Utilizing a top 10 party slicer, the dashboard allows for filtering and comparing performance across all major political parties. Detailed analysis of party trends facilitates a better understanding of their influence and strategic positioning over time.

Voting Metrics

+

The dashboard features a speed meter for total voting polls and a donut chart breaking down total voting between elevators and voters. These visuals provide quick insights into overall voter engagement and the efficiency of the electoral process.

Candidate & Demographics

+

Detailed candidate information is presented via a scatter chart and an interactive candidate table. Additionally, demographic insights are provided through a male/female donut chart and a caste separation donut chart (ST, SC, General), uncovering patterns in candidate profiles and voter demographics.
